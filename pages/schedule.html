SCHEDULE

There are two classes during the week. You are more than welcome to join for a single class on one of those days.
To attend a class it is mandatory to reserve a spot.

*Please note: When you have ilness symptomps you cannot attend a class, thanks for your understanding

TIMETABLE
Tuesday: 19:30 - 21:00
Wednesday: 20:15 - 21:45
(Attend 10 mins before the class)

LOCATION:
The Beatzone
Poeldijkstraat 391 Amsterdam

CLOTHING:
Wear clothing that you feel comfortable with.
Make sure that your shoes are clean and have a good grip.
Don't forget a towel because you will get wet from the sweat!
Take a bottle of water with you to stay hydrated.
