ABOUT FITDANCE

Brazilian FitDance is a conditioning training where every week new movements to one.
Brazilian funk song is played on repeat for one and a half hour with one break in between.

Participants start with a few movements and slowly more are added every round and everything is repeated to the same song until the time is up.
