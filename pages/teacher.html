The teacher

Hi i am Diego 32 years old and from Brazil.
When I was young, I grew up listening and dancing to funk music in my country.
I started teaching the Brazilian funk dancing moves as a hobby for a couple of friends.

Now I am able to teach others to do this and I love it!
The choreography is all made up by myself and I put all my effort and dedication into doing this.
Dancing is fun, healthy, and it brings so much joy in life that I like to share with everybody who wants to learn!
